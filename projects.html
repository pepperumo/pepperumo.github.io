Genetic Algorithm for Structural Optimization

AI Genetic Algorithms CAD FEM

Developed an AI-driven optimization framework using genetic algorithms to automatically generate and refine mechanical component designs that meet specific performance criteria and material constraints.

Key Features

  • Multi-objective optimization for balancing weight, strength, and cost
  • Integration with CAD systems for automated design generation
  • Automated FEM analysis loop for performance validation
  • Parameter encoding for complex mechanical structures

Technologies

Python, DEAP, CAD API integration (CATIA), ANSYS for FEM analysis, Custom evolutionary operators

Achieved 25% weight reduction in critical components while maintaining structural integrity and manufacturing feasibility. Solution converged 60% faster than traditional optimization approaches.

Reinforcement Learning for Robotic Control

Reinforcement Learning Robotics ROS2

Key Features

Floating Water Turbine System

Sustainable Energy Fluid Mechanics CAD Design

Designed a floating water turbine nacelle system for energy generation or water pumping in underprivileged regions, focusing on durability, efficiency, and ease of maintenance with minimal infrastructure requirements.

Key Features

  • Self-stabilizing floating platform design
  • Optimized turbine blade geometry for low-flow conditions
  • Modular construction for easy assembly and maintenance
  • Integrated mechanical water pump option or electrical generator

Technologies

CATIA for 3D modeling, ANSYS for fluid flow and structural simulation, Sustainable materials analysis

Results

The prototype demonstrated energy generation capabilities in water flows as low as 0.5m/s, with potential to provide water pumping or electricity for small communities with minimal maintenance requirements.
